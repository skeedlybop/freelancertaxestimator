Contact Us

We read every message and aim to reply within 2–3 business days.

Whether you've found an error in one of our country guides, have an idea for a feature, want us to add a country, or are interested in a partnership, we'd love to hear from you.

Email us directly at

contact@freelancertaxestimator.com

What to reach out about

  • Corrections & updates — a rate, threshold, or deadline that's out of date or wrong. Please include a link to the official source if you have one.
  • Feature requests — something you wish the calculator or guides did.
  • New countries — tell us where you freelance and we'll consider adding it.
  • Partnerships & advertising — relevant tools and services for freelancers.
  • Privacy questions — anything about how your data is handled (see our Privacy Policy).

What we can't help with

We're a free educational tool, not a tax practice, so we're unable to give personalised tax advice, prepare or file returns, or comment on your specific financial situation. For that, please consult a qualified accountant or tax adviser in your country. Our About page explains how to find a good one.
