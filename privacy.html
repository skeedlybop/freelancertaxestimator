Last updated: May 2025

FreelancerTaxEstimator.com is a client-side tool. All calculations are performed in your browser — we do not collect, store, or transmit any of the financial figures you enter into the calculator.

We may collect standard, non-personally-identifiable information automatically via third-party services, including:
